Ugali Matumbo recipe

Ugali Matumbo
Great recipe for Ugali Matumbo

Ingredients:

  • 500 grms maize flour
  • 2 bunches kales
  • 500 grms matumbo
  • 2 ripe tomatoes
  • 1 large onion

Steps:

  1. Cut the onion into pieces
  2. Add cooking fat and place on fire
  3. Wait for 5 mins then add sliced tomatoes
  4. Add boiled matumbo and cook for 30 mins
  5. Steam the kales and serve while warm

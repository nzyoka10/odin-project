Githeri Recipe

Great recipe for Githeri

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of mixed dried maize and beans (Githeri mix)
  • Water for soaking
  • 2-3 cups of water for cooking
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Place the mixed dried maize and beans (Githeri mix) in a large bowl and rinse them thoroughly under cold running water.
  2. Transfer the rinsed Githeri mix to a pot or large saucepan and cover it with water. Allow it to soak for at least 4-6 hours or overnight.
  3. After soaking, drain the water and rinse the Githeri mix again.
  4. In the same pot or saucepan, add 2-3 cups of fresh water and bring the Githeri mix to a boil. Reduce the heat to a simmer and cook for about 45 minutes to 1 hour or until the maize and beans are tender but not mushy. Add more water if needed during cooking.
  5. In a separate pan, heat the cooking oil over medium heat.
  6. Add the cumin seeds and sauté for a minute or until they start to sizzle and release their aroma.
  7. Add the chopped onion and garlic to the pan and sauté until they become translucent.
  8. Stir in the chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften and the mixture becomes a thick sauce.
  9. Add the chopped green and red bell peppers (if using) and cook for another 2-3 minutes until they are tender.
  10. Season the tomato and pepper mixture with paprika, salt, and pepper to taste.
  11. Combine the cooked Githeri mix with the tomato and pepper sauce in the large pot. Mix well and let it simmer together for a few more minutes to meld the flavors.
  12. Your Githeri is now ready to serve. Enjoy it as is or with some crusty bread!
